The Reality of the OHIP Waiting Period

Most newcomers don't realize the financial risk until it's too late.

  • No OHIP = Private Billing

    Until your card arrives, hospitals bill you as a private patient at premium rates.

  • ER Visits are Costly

    A basic emergency visit can cost $800–$1,200 just for the initial physician assessment.

  • Unexpected Stays

    Overnight hospital stays often exceed $3,000 per day, not including tests or medication.

  • The Critical Gap

    Health issues don't wait for your paperwork. Protection is needed from day one.
